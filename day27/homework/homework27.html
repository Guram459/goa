Jean-Claude Van Damme

Jean -Claude Van Damme ( born Jean-Claude Camille François Van Varenberg , Dutch . and French Jean-Claude Camille François Van Varenberg ; nickname - Muscles from Brussels, Muscles from Brussels , English The Muscles from Brussels ; born October 18, 1960 [ 1 ] [ 2 ] , Berchem-Sainte-Agathe , Brussels-Capital Region ) is an American actor , director , screenwriter , fight choreographer and producer of Belgian ( Flemish ) origin; bodybuilder , martial artist . In 1978, he took third place in his weight category at the amateur European Bodybuilding Championships , and a year later, competing at the Belgian Championships, called "Mr. Belgium", he won in his and the main weight categories, thus becoming the absolute champion [ 3 ] [ 4 ] [ 5 ] [ 6 ] . He is the 1979 European Champion in karate and kickboxing in the middleweight category among professionals [ 4 ] [ 7 ] [ 8 ] [ 9 ] [ 10 ] , and also has a black belt [ 4 ] [ 5 ] [ 7 ] . During his fighting career (karate and kickboxing), he had a total of 55 fights, in which he won 53 times (50 of them by knockout , 3 by technical knockout ) and lost 2 times by judges' decision. He was also a three-time world champion [ 6 ] [ 8 ] . During his acting career, he has appeared in more than 50 films, including: Bloodsport ( 1988), Kickboxer (1989), AWOL (1990), Death Warrant (1990 ), Double Impact (1991), Universal Soldier (1992) and its sequel Universal Soldier 2: The Return (1999), Nowhere to Run (1993), Hard Target (1993), Timecop (1994), Street Fighter (1994 ), Sudden Death (1995), Legionnaire (1998), Replicant (2001), Death Wake (2004), J.C.V.D. ( 2008), The Expendables 2 (2012), " Close Enemies " (2013) [ 11 ] [ 12 ] [ 13 ] . In 1996, Van Damme made his directorial debut with the martial arts film In Search of Adventure , in which he also starred and co-wrote the story. Also in 2020, additional filming was completed on Van Damme's second directorial effort, the action drama Frenchie , which he directed and produced from his own script and starred himself.
